Veggie Plate

Ratatouille

Equally good when served hot as a vegetable, or cold as an appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 1 medium Onion
  • 1 medium Eggplant
  • 2 medium Zucchini
  • 1 small Green Pepper
  • 1 clove Garlic
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 (8-oz)Tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare vegetables for cooking.
  2. Saute all vegetables, except tomatoes, in hot oil in T-FAL Pressure Cooker over moderate heat until just tender, stirring frequently.
  3. Add tomatoes,water,salt,and pepper.
  4. Cover, seal, and bring to full pressure.
  5. Cook 10 minutes.
  6. Release steam quickly.
  7. If there is any excess liquid left in cooker, cook, stirring frequently, over moderate heat until liquid is reduced.
  8. Adjust seasonings.
  9. Serve hot, at room temperature, or chilled, as desired.
